My most derest and best belowyd brother I humbly recommend vn to yowr
grace.  MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.4

Sir so yt tys that I have bene very seke and eke atons for the wyche
I was fayne to sende for Master Peter the fesysyon for to have $holpen
{TEXT:hoplen} me of the dessays that I haue.  MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.5

How be yt I am rathar wors than better.  Were for I trowst showrly to
come vp to Londone with my Lord.  MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.6

For and yf I shold tary here I am sowr I shold never asspeare the sekenys
that I haue.  Wer for, Sir, I wolde be the gladther a grete dele to
com thether, by cawse I wold be glad to se yowr grace, the wyche I do
thyncke long for to do; MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.7

for I haue been a grete wyle out of yowr syte: MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.8

and now I thowst I shal not be so long agene: MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.9

for the syte of yowr grace ys to me the grettys comforte to me that may
be possybel.  MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,304.10

No <P_1.1,305> more to your grace at thys tyme,
MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,305.11

bwt I pray God send yow yowr hartys dessyr, and showrly to the syte
of yow.  MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,305.12

by yowr lowyng suster Mary the Frenche Qu..  MTUDOR-1520-E1-P2,1.1,305.13
